Greenpoint Capital is the investment vehicle run by Alex Cohen and Bart Macdonald.

We invest between $50k and $150k in pre-seed through Series A startups. If we don't already know the founders, we like to see:

  • Product velocity
  • Early customer validation / traction
  • Ability to hire and rally a team

The areas of focus that we invest in are primarily:

  • Digital health and healthcare infrastructure
  • B2B SaaS and Prosumer SaaS (industry agnostic here)
  • Future of financial services (primarily B2B)
  • AI that augments any of the above

We do make exceptions, although it's rare.
